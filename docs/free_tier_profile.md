# Free-tier profile — what $0 reproduces, exactly

> **Status: reference (drafted 2026-07-18).** The mandate is bounded-data-budget
> by construction (SPEC §1: free tiers by default; named, ratified purchases
> only — and the one evaluated purchase was reversed in favor of accumulation,
> docs/data_purchase_evaluation.md §6). This file makes "usable for free" a
> specified claim instead of a vibe: which capabilities a clean checkout plus
> free keys actually reproduces, which it cannot, and why.

## 1. The free stack

| Source | Tier facts | What it feeds |
|---|---|---|
| Alpaca paper account | free; IEX feed; paper venue; corporate-actions API works on the paper key | the entire live loop: bars, orders, fills, dividends wedge |
| Twelve Data | free key: 8 req/min, 800/day (`prism/io/rate_limit.py`) | the research spine's bar/dividend caches |
| FRED | free key | the regime layer's macro series |
| SEC EDGAR, Nasdaq directories, SSGA daily holdings | free, no key | the observatory's membership capture (S&P 500/400 via SPY/MDY holdings) |
| Alpha Vantage | free key, 25 req/day | the observatory's active+delisted listing universe (monthly) |

## 2. Reproducible at $0 today

- **The paper loop, end to end** (docs/quickstart.md): Alpaca keys only. Bars
  come from the broker's own IEX feed; no Twelve Data key needed.
- **The preflight, replay, and diagnostics**: `prism-doctor`, the replay
  instrument over local caches, the concordance/equity/fills ledgers.
- **The in-flight membership data**: point-in-time universe files ship
  in-tree (`data/universe/`), regenerated by the observatory's free capture.
- **The research spine's caches, slowly**: ~570 member histories at 800
  requests/day rebuild over a few days of scripted fetching — the loader's
  token bucket and 429 backoff pace this within the tier.

## 3. Not reproducible at $0 — recorded, not hidden

- **Pre-2020 delisted mid/large-cap prices.** No known free source; registered
  as the knowing price of the zero-budget stance in the replication
  pre-registration (docs/replication_preregistration.md §8) — the C2/C3
  crash-era cells stay unscheduled until a source exists or is accumulated.
- **Delisted-ticker bars generally.** The standing fact: the certified
  backtests carry zero delisted-ticker price rows. The forward fix is
  accumulation, not purchase (docs/dead_ticker_price_design.md).
- **Bond-ETF bars on the Twelve Data free tier.** The vendor returns zero
  bars for bond ETFs at this tier (measured 2026-07-18 during trend-universe
  resolution, docs/trend_design.md §1); the trend program's credit buckets
  read the Alpaca feed instead.
- **Sub-daily data, consolidated-tape feeds, and anything requiring
  redistribution rights.** Out of mandate (SPEC §1); the IEX feed's ~5%
  median share is an accepted execution-eligibility constraint (MARKETS.md).

## 4. The doctrine

The budget line is not an inconvenience to route around; it is part of the
evidence. A result reproducible only with paid data is a different claim than
one reproducible at $0, and the claim tiers require knowing which one is being
made (SPEC §10). When a gap matters — pre-2020 delisted prices — the program
records it as a named limitation with a trigger for revisiting, rather than
silently quietly narrowing the question (docs/replication_preregistration.md
§8 is the worked example).
